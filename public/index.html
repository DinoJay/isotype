Isotype Visualization

This is my take on Isotype visualization. It is not really a consistent translation of the infographic style developed by Otto Neurath, Gerd Arntz and others at the 'Gesellschafts- und Wirtschaftsmuseum' in Wien. I just used pictograms by Gerd Arntz to polish some standard d3 example. Orginally, I intended also to use icons for Income and people, but turned it down because I like the visual appeal of circles. In a second iteration icons may come. To ackknowledge, this is only an experiment and not real piece of information visualization. It does not hold all standards how to present data in a meaningful way. The data comes from www.census.gov and basically shows the distribution of income among graduates in their first jobs (categorized by degrees). But for the rest let the data speak!
